set_master_control (volume_graph)
Link the widget to the master channel of your system (uses amixer). a left clic toggle mute/unmute, wheel up to increase the volume and wheel down to decrease the volume

Parameters:

  • volume_graph the volume graph

Usage:

    myvolume:set_master_control()
new (args)
Create a volume_graph widget.

Parameters:

  • args Standard widget() arguments. You should add width and height key to set graph geometry.

Returns:

    A graph widget.
